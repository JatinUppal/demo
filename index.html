Services

The SDC creates surveys for clients and internal teams that meet the firms security and confidentiality standards. We can guide you through the online development of questionnaires, survey distribution, response collection and analysis. More
  • Completely customizable design of online survey including the ability to match client design guidelines or embedding within client Intranet.
  • Secure – Survey platform hosted within McKinsey’s network and all data can be encrypted.
  • Interactive surveys can include pictures, video and animations.
  • Fully compatible with iPhones, iPads, Blackberry and Android devices.
  • Support for securely hosting highly data-intensive and complex surveys for very large audiences (e.g. market research survey for >100k participants)
  • Scalable infrastructure – No limit on number of participants or survey size.
  • Support for more than 150 languages
  • Functionality like Page/question/section randomization, complex calculations and indexing is possible.
  • One question polls with instant result graphs can be created and embedded to external websites.
  • Results output in Excel, SPSS or text format. Off-the-shelf data cuts available.
  • Real-time response downloads, Online data visualization, Reporting, Analysis and automated Powerpoint/Excel/Pdf exports are also available.
  • Consolidated analysis for multiple surveys or historical data benchmarking for repetitive surveys.
